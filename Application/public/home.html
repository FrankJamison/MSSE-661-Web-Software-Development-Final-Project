Welcome to AncientWhiteArmyVet's RPG Tools!

Here we present a number of tools for you to use during character generation in the 5th Edition of Dungeons & Dragons.

The tools here include a random physical stat generator that covers all of the official races available to date to determine the height, weight, and age of your character; an ability generator that uses the roll 4d6 and drop lowest paradigm; and a slew of pre-generated characters for you to choose from if you just want to pick up a character sheet and go.

Use the menu at the top right of the page to select a tool!
